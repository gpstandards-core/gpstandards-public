Sovereign Privacy Protocol

Last Updated: January 15, 2026

At Genesis & Prime Standards LLC ("G&P Standards," "we," "us," or "our"), we operate on a philosophy of Data Sovereignty. This Privacy Protocol outlines our rigid data handling practices, our "Zero-Training" covenant, and your rights as a Sovereign Partner. This policy applies to our website, our Digital Worker infrastructure, and our proprietary APIs.

1. The "Zero-Training" Covenant

1.1 No Model Training. We definitively state that G&P Standards does not use Customer Data (including case files, rent rolls, or client communications) to train, fine-tune, or improve our foundational Large Language Models (LLMs) or those of our third-party providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic).

1.2 Isolation. All inference tasks are executed in isolated, ephemeral containers. Once a session is closed or a "Digital Worker" completes its task, the context window is purged from memory. No data persists in the inference layer.

2. Information We Collect

We collect strictly limited categories of data to facilitate the "Secure Uplink":

  • Account Authority Data: Names, corporate emails, and IP addresses of authorized personnel (Admins/Associates) accessing the G&P Console.
  • System Telemetry: Anonymized technical logs (e.g., latency, error rates, uptime status) used solely for forensic debugging and infrastructure stability.
  • Financial Data: We do not store full credit card numbers. All payment processing is handled by Stripe or our invoiced banking partners (Delaware C-Corp Accounts).

3. How We Process Data (Architecture)

Our infrastructure is designed to minimize exposure ("Attack Surface").

  • Region: Unless otherwise requested, all processing occurs within the United States (US-East-4 or US-Gov-Virginia).
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 and in transit using TLS 1.3. Keys are managed via Azure Key Vault.
  • Vector Storage: Customer Knowledge Bases (RAG) are stored in dedicated vector namespaces. Data is never commingled between clients.

4. Authorized Subprocessors

To deliver Sovereign Intelligence, we engage specific high-trust subprocessors. All have signed Data Processing Addendums (DPAs) enforcing Zero Data Retention (ZDR):

  • Microsoft Azure Government: Cloud Hosting & Compute.
  • OpenAI Enterprise: Inference API (Zero Retention Policy Active).
  • Pinecone / Weaviate: Vector Database Infrastructure.
  • Ketch: Consent Management Platform.

5. Data Egress & Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data. Data only leaves the secure enclave under two conditions:

5.1 Client Directive. When your authorized user explicitly instructs a Digital Worker to email a report or file a document externally.

5.2 Legal Compulsion. If compelled by a valid subpoena, court order, or search warrant issued by a competent United States court. We will attempt to notify you of such requests unless prohibited by law.

6. Your Rights (CCPA, GDPR, VCDPA)

Regardless of your location, we extend high-standard rights to all Sovereign Partners:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all telemetry data linked to your account.
  • Right to Erasure ("The Kill Switch"): Request the permanent deletion of your vector indexes and account data. We execute this within 48 hours of verification.
  • Right to Opt-Out: You may opt out of non-essential cookies via our Ketch Consent Manager.
